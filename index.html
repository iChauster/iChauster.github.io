About Ivan

Hey There, I'm Ivan Chau.
I'm a rising junior at Montgomery High School, New Jersey, and I'm extremely interested and passionate about Computer Science. I love developing apps and products for others to use, and, more importantly, making an impact on the society I live in.
I've worked with iOS Development (Swift & Obj-C), Web Development in (NodeJS, Express, Jade, CSS, HTML), Android Development (Java), and also work with databases (MongoDB). I'm eager to learn more to make better and greater things!
Much of my time is focused on spreading my love for Computer Science with peers and students. I'm on the board for my school's Computer Science Club, and am currently proposing our first annual Hackathon, MontyHacks. I highly subscribe to the idea that one of the best ways to learn things is through using and experimenting with them.
Aside from my time spent developing apps, I'm an avid Ultimate Frisbee player, (Come Support Us!) and listen to lots of music. (Daft Punk, Porter Robinson, Madeon, Mura Masa, and Kanye West are some among my favorites.)
